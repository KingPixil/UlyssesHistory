    A Brief Summary of what Happend.

    The Adventures of Ulysses, is a heart-stopping, jaw-dropping tale, with adventure at every corner. Join Ulysses as he fights monsters, sails the oceans, and tries to make his way back home to Ithaca. He is faced with cyclopes, cannibal giants, and sorceresses. Love and violence cloud his mind, but he trudges through, with Ithaca in his sights. The action and adventure in this book, will leave you on the edge of your seat.

He is famous tricking the people of Troy. He and his army were inside of a huge wooden horse, and his army put it outside of troy.
